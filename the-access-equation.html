01 — My Hope
The best surgeon, the best teacher, the best lawyer — their knowledge shouldn't be locked behind a price tag.

AI compresses the cost of expertise toward zero. A medical student in rural India can train on the same simulation as someone at Johns Hopkins. A first-generation college student can get tutoring as good as any prep school. The gap between the best institutions and everyone else is collapsing — and it should.

02 — AI as a force for good
Healthcare and education. These are where the access gap changes lives — and ends them.
🏥
Healthcare
A misdiagnosis in a rural clinic isn't a billing error — it's a death sentence. AI-powered diagnostics and training tools don't just save money. They save the people that the current system forgot about.

Education
The difference between a student who gets personalized mentorship and one who doesn't isn't grades — it's trajectory. AI makes one-on-one guidance scalable for the first time in history.
